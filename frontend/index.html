What it offers

Hands-free control

Move through your computer with head movement and blink actions designed for everyday accessibility support.

Voice guided

Speak commands naturally after the wake word — the assistant handles the rest with safe, reversible actions.

Local and private

Everything runs on your machine. No cloud, no data sent anywhere, no internet required during use.
